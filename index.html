About Parkchester Medical

For Over 35 Years, Parkchester Medical Has Provided Expert Evaluation and Treatment

  • Our Background

    Parkchester Medical has been serving the community for over 30 years as a patient-first private practice with a commitment to the highest standards of care. We provide excellent medical care efficiently, and participate in nearly all insurance plans in NYC.

  • Electronic Medical Records

    Parkchester Medical utilizes the latest in electronic medical records so all of your history and records can be accessed in moments. This approach provides more thorough and comprehensive care by our professional staff.

  • Our Mission

    To provide our community with exceptional medical treatment by experienced board-certified physicians in a safe convenient and comfortable environment without delay.

  • Why Choose Us?

    Our goal is to provide you with exceptional care and personal attention immediately. Unlike most clinics, at Parkchester Medical, you will be seen by a board-certified physician with years of experience. We are committed to a new model of medicine that ensures the higest standards of care for adults and pediatric patients. Our commitment to these standards has led to several Certifications of Excellence by the Department of Health and other government agencies.

    Our staff speaks English, Spanish, and Bengali. Translations for other languages are available instantly via our Language Bank services.
